ChordPlayer by Ryan Isler.

HOW TO USE:
1. Create "chords.txt" in same directory as ChordPlayer.exe
2. your txt should look something like this (without the quotes):
"A B C D Bb g A E E A _ _ f g Ab A "
3. Change the notes, add rests, and make some BEEP-BOP BOP music!

FEATURES:
- two octaves: ab - g, Ab - G
- uses COMPUTER BEEPS!
- Make some sweet music!
